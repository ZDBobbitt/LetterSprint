How to Play

The Grid

A 4×4 grid of random letters appears each round. Each letter carries a point value shown in the corner of the tile.

Forming Words

Click and drag through adjacent tiles to spell a word. Words must be at least 4 letters.

The Goal

Each round you have 60 seconds to score at least 20 points.

Rounds

When you finish a round, the number of seconds left in the round represents your total points scored that round. The game ends when you fail to finish a round.
